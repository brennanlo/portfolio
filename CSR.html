CSR Templates — Streamlining insurance claim processes

Company / WSIB Innovation Lab
Role / 1 of 2 Designers
Tools / Sketch & Invision
Timeline / 3 Weeks

Overview

The Lab has been tasked to help with the redesign of the template process at the Kitchener Call Center. Currently, the templates are difficult to fill out and export, non-intuitive, and confusing to draw information from. The instructions for choosing which template to use and how to use them are excessive, lengthy, and difficult to navigate.  All content and information is being provided by Kristen, the Kitchener office CSR Manager, and the design team is focusing on improving the form flow and interface design.
